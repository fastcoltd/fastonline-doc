Service rule

Service rules

ReadsReads：April 02, 2025

1. Introduction

FASTRESP ("we," "us," or "our") operates a virtual goods trading platform at fastresp.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our Site, including features such as user accounts, stores, blogs, articles, comments, payments, demands (bidding), and inventory management. We are committed to complying with applicable data protection laws worldwide, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL) of China, and others. By using the Site, you consent to the practices described herein, subject to your rights under applicable law.

2. Scope and Applicability

This policy applies to all users of the Site, including buyers, sellers, and visitors, regardless of location. It covers personal data collected through our website, mobile applications, and related services. It does not apply to data collected offline or by third-party sites linked from our Site.

3. Data Controller Information

FASTRESP, located at 123 Virtual Lane, Digital City, DC 00000, is the data controller responsible for your personal information. For GDPR purposes, our EU representative can be contacted at eu-privacy@fastresp.com. For China’s PIPL, our local representative is at china-privacy@fastresp.com. For South Korea’s PIPA, our local agent is at korea-privacy@fastresp.com.

4. Information We Collect

4.1 Personal Information You Provide

4.2 Information Collected Automatically

4.2.1 Usage Data: Pages visited, items viewed, search queries, time spent, and clickstream data.

4.2.2 Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and geolocation data (approximate, based on IP).

4.2.3 Inventory Data: Stock levels and product details submitted by sellers.

4.3 Information from Third Parties

4.3.1 Social media profile data if you log in via third-party authentication (e.g., Google, Facebook).

4.3.2 Analytics data from partners like Google Analytics.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on the following legal grounds:

5.1 Contract: To fulfill our agreement with you (e.g., account creation, transactions, bidding).

5.2 Consent: Where you opt-in (e.g., marketing emails, non-essential cookies).

5.3 Legitimate Interests: To improve our services, prevent fraud, and ensure Site functionality.

5.4 Legal Obligation: To comply with laws, such as tax reporting or responding to legal requests.

6. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

6.1.1 Operate and improve the Site, including user accounts, stores, and bidding systems.

6.1.2 Process payments, manage inventory, and deliver virtual goods.

6.1.3 Publish your comments, articles, and demands as intended.

6.1.4 Send transactional emails (e.g., order confirmations) and, with consent, marketing communications.

6.1.5 Analyze usage trends and personalize content (e.g., recommended items).

6.1.6 Detect and prevent fraud, enforce our Rules of Service, and ensure security.

6.1.7 Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

7. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

7.1.1 Service Providers: Third parties for payment processing (e.g., Stripe), hosting, analytics (e.g., Google Analytics), and customer support.

7.1.2 Other Users: Your username, store details, comments, posts, and demands are visible to others as part of Site functionality.

7.1.3 Legal Authorities: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

7.1.4 Business Transfers: In case of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

7.1.5 Advertising Partners: With your consent, for personalized ads. You may opt-out via your account settings or by contacting us.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes without your explicit consent. California residents can opt-out of data sales via our “Do Not Sell My Personal Information” link on the homepage.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

8.1 What Are Cookies and Tracking Technologies?

8.1.1 Cookies are small files stored on your device. We also use pixels, web beacons, and similar tools to collect data.

8.2 Types of Cookies We Use

8.2.1 Essential Cookies: Required for Site operation (e.g., login, cart, bidding functionality).

8.2.2 Preference Cookies: Store your settings (e.g., language, currency).

8.2.3 Analytics Cookies: Measure usage (e.g., Google Analytics) to improve the Site.

8.2.4 Marketing Cookies: Deliver personalized ads via third-party networks.

8.3 Cookie Duration

8.3.1 Session Cookies: Expire when you close your browser.

8.3.2 Persistent Cookies: Remain until deleted or expired (up to 2 years).

8.4 Managing Cookies

8.4.1 Use our Cookie Settings popup or your browser settings to manage preferences. Non-essential cookies (e.g., analytics, marketing) are only set with your explicit consent.

8.4.2 Disabling essential cookies may impair Site functionality.

8.4.3 For more, see our Cookie Policy.

8.5 Third-Party Tracking

8.5.1 Third parties (e.g., ad networks, analytics providers) may use cookies. Review their policies for details.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary:

9.1.1 Account Data: Until you delete your account, plus a 30-day grace period, unless legally required longer.

9.1.2 Transaction Data: 7 years for tax and accounting purposes.

9.1.3 Content: Comments and posts remain indefinitely unless removed by you or us.

9.1.4 Analytics Data: Up to 26 months, then anonymized.

9.1.5 Inactive accounts may be deleted after 2 years of inactivity.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your jurisdiction, including the United States, where our servers are located. For China’s PIPL, we comply with Cybersecurity Administration requirements if applicable. We ensure compliance with laws like GDPR through:

10.1.1 Standard Contractual Clauses: Agreements with third parties to protect your data.

10.1.2 Adequacy Decisions: Transfers to countries recognized as having adequate protections.

10.1.3 You may request details by contacting us.

11. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have these rights:

11.1.1 Access: Obtain a copy of your data.

11.1.2 Rectification: Correct inaccurate data via your account or support.

11.1.3 Erasure: Request deletion, subject to legal retention (e.g., tax records).

11.1.4 Restriction: Limit processing in certain cases (e.g., disputes).

11.1.5 Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.

11.1.6 Object: Opt-out of marketing or processing based on legitimate interests.

11.1.7 Withdraw Consent: Revoke consent at any time (e.g., via Cookie Settings).

11.1.8 CCPA Rights: California residents may request disclosure of data sold/shared and opt-out of sales via our “Do Not Sell My Personal Information” link.

11.1.9 To exercise these rights, email privacy@fastresp.com. We respond within 30 days (or 45 for CCPA, if extended).

11.1.10 You may lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (e.g., ICO in the UK, CNIL in France, NPA in China).

12. Data Security

We use technical and organizational measures (e.g., encryption, access controls, regular audits) to protect your data. In case of a data breach, we will notify you and relevant authorities immediately, or within 72 hours where required by law (e.g., GDPR, Thailand PDPA, PIPA). However, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for securing your account credentials.

13. Third-Party Links and Services

The Site may link to third-party websites (e.g., payment processors, social media). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their policies before use.

14. Children’s Privacy

The Site is not intended for children under 16 (or 13 in the US per COPPA). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we learn of such data, we will delete it. Contact us if you believe a child has provided us with information.

15. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as needed. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last update" date. Material changes will be notified via email or Site banners at least 7 days in advance (30 days for GDPR/CCPA significant changes).

16. Contact Us

For questions, concerns, or to exercise your rights, please contact us using the following details:

16.1.1 Email: privacy@fastresp.com

16.1.2 Mail: FASTRESP Privacy Team, 123 Virtual Lane, Digital City, DC 00000

16.1.3 EU Representative (GDPR): eu-privacy@fastresp.com

16.1.4 China Representative (PIPL): china-privacy@fastresp.com

16.1.5 South Korea Agent (PIPA): korea-privacy@fastresp.com

16.1.6 Phone: +1-800-FASTRESP (Available 9 AM - 5 PM EST, Monday to Friday)

16.1.7 Data Protection Officer (DPO): For GDPR-related inquiries, contact our DPO at dpo@fastresp.com

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 30 days, in compliance with applicable laws. For Southeast Asia (e.g., Thailand, Singapore), contact our regional support at sea-privacy@fastresp.com.
